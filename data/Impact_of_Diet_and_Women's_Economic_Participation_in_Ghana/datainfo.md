# Title: Impact of Diet and Women's Economic Participation in Ghana

## Description:
This dataset explores the relationship between infrastructure, diet, and women's economic participation in Ghana. It includes a variety of demographic, economic, and social variables collected from households across different regions of Ghana. The data spans the years 2016 and 2017 and aims to provide insights into how infrastructure and dietary habits influence women's economic activities and overall household well-being.
