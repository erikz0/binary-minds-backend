# Title: Impact of Road Infrastructure in Rural Nigeria

## Description:
This dataset explores the impact of road infrastructure on various socio-economic factors in rural Nigeria. It includes data collected from 1233 respondents across multiple states and zones, covering aspects such as household size, income, agricultural practices, asset ownership, and access to financial services. The dataset aims to provide insights into how road infrastructure influences rural livelihoods and economic activities.
